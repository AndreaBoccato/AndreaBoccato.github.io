Faqs

Where my data will be stored?
Your data will be securely stored in the cloud. This means that you can have your data on every device you log into with the same account.

How can i buy the pro version?
You can buy the pro version in the app settings. This is a very low priced subscription to support development and server costs, as well as unlock great new features.
